# Logic

For complex forms, it is often necessary to incorporate logic to enhance functionality. This may include displaying or hiding specific fields based on certain conditions, or making a field mandatory only under particular circumstances.

The `Logic` array is designed to hold all the conditions and actions required to dynamically modify the form according to these specific scenarios.
